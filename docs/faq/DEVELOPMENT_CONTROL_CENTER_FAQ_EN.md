# FAQ — Development Control Center (EN)

## Where is the roadmap?

**Roadmap** tab in the Development Control Center. Data from `/api/dev-dashboard/roadmap`.

## Why is SSH disabled?

**SSH disabled** is **intended** in the lab MVP and shown as a safe state, not an error.

## Why no public uploads?

Public rescue auto-upload stays blocked. **Disabled** is correct.

## What is the telemetry server?

The **Development Server** (`local_lab`) — ingest-only, read-only SSH prepared but disabled.

## Can I build an ISO from here?

No. ISO build status stays **pending** until real build evidence exists.

## Are documentation counts live?

Read-only scan of `docs/` — no hardcoded numbers.
